# 🏠 Rent or Buy? Real Estate Analysis in Northern France 🇫🇷

## 📌 Project Goal
This project explores whether it is more cost-effective to **rent or buy** a home in **Northern France**, using open real estate data (DVF, CLAMEUR, INSEE). We analyze **return on investment (ROI)**, **payback period**, and **regional trends** across different property types and price segments.

---

## 🌍 Region of Study: Northern France

- **Regions covered:** Hauts-de-France, Normandie
- **Departments included:** 02, 59, 60, 62, 80, 14, 27, 50, 61, 76

---

## 🗂 Data Sources
- [DVF – Demande de Valeurs Foncières](https://www.data.gouv.fr/fr/datasets/demandes-de-valeurs-foncieres/)
- [CLAMEUR / Observatoire des loyers](https://www.data.gouv.fr/fr/datasets/observatoire-des-loyers/)
- [INSEE](https://www.insee.fr) – for demographics and household income
- [impots.gouv.fr](https://www.impots.gouv.fr) – for tax and notary cost references

---

## 🏘️ Property Types Analyzed

- 🏢 Apartments (T1, T2, T3…)
- 🏡 Houses
- 🆕 New vs Old properties
- 💰 Price categories: Low-cost, Mid-range, Luxury

---

## 📊 Key Questions

1. **Is it financially better to rent or buy?**
2. **How many years of rent would equal the total cost of purchase?**
3. **What is the ROI of buying and renting out a property?**
4. **Which type of property pays off faster?**
5. **How does location affect returns?**
